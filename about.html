Senior High School Tracks

Offer programs that equip students with the skills and knowledge needed for their chosen careers. Example:

  • STEM - Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics
  • ABM - Accountancy, Business, and Management
  • HUMSS - Humanities and Social Sciences
  • TVL-ICT - Information and Communications Technology

Teaching Faculty

Our teachers are dedicated professionals who guide students through classroom discussions, laboratory activities, and real-world projects. They encourage critical thinking, creativity, and lifelong learning while helping students achieve their full potential.

Student Outcomes and Career Paths

After completing Senior High School, students may:

  • Continue to college
  • Earn industry certifications
  • Apply for entry-level jobs
  • Start small businesses
  • Develop technical and leadership skills
